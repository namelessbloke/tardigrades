TARDIGRADES

fasinating tiny beings

Tardigrades, water bears, Moss piglet Closely related to arthropods, (show picture of arthropods) not in the same family though, tardigrades belong to the family of phylum Tardigrada which is there Latin name and means “slow stepper” and there is a bit more than 1100 species that have been discovered so far. The first of which was discovered by the German zoologist Johann August Ephraim Goeze.
